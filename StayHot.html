Your coffee is probably getting cold right now

Fun fact - the harder you work, the faster your coffee or tea cools down.
Also, the lively the conversation at the family table, the quicker the dishes get cold.

Combine, StayHot, Enjoy

StayHot teapot warmer can be combined depending on the situation, quantity and size of the dish.

Expandable to the required size on the table and, once used, stackable for compact storage.

StayHot can be manufactured of heat-resistant ceramic, concrete or borosilicate glass.
