"Where can I charge?"

Often one of our first thoughts in a new space - visiting, an office, a conference or a hotel...
This experience led to the idea of a lamp that shares light and energy.

The minimalistic form of the Pi-Light is defined by the plug & socket type.
While its shape is described by circle, its length of 314mm is a kind of metaphor for the relationship between shapes and dimensions.

Light up, charge, combine

The double-layered canopy design allows the pendant to be mounted in the classic way, but can also be used as a cord support.
Just create different combinations and arrange the lamps according to the situation.

Hanging, against a wall or on a table?

Additional accessories such as adjustable diffusers, rotating clip and base extend the possibilities of use.
