RUB Design Book


 Published in Italy on Jan, 2014

RUB DESIGN BOOK is the first publishing project which connects Ukrainian, Belarusian and Russian designers with the European manufacturers to give innovative design proposals and open new markets.

Serge's projects have been published among 65 designers and studios profiles.
The Book includes 158 projects, more than 500 images, interviews and biorgaphies.


RUB Design Pavillon in Milan

The Book with selected projects were exhibited for the first time at Russian Design Pavillon in Milan.

Over the years RUB DESIGN turned into fresh phenomenon, without the obligatory Soviet iconography.
It's introduction dedicated to the cultural and historical origins of Ukrainain, Belarusian and Russian design and its perspectives.
